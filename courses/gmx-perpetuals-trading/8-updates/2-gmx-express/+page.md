# GMX Express

See [here](https://docs.gmx.io/docs/trading#express-trading-and-one-click-trading) for details

## Overview
GMX Express Trading is one of the trading modes available on GMX that improves the speed and experience of executing trades.

---

## Key Features
- **Off-chain signing**
  - Instead of making you sign every trade transaction on-chain through your wallet, Express allows you to sign a message locally. GMX then broadcasts your trade on-chain via a relay (Gelato).
- **Premium RPC**
  - Transactions are sent to GMX-sponsored premium RPCs
- **Flexibility in gas payments**
  - USDC or WETH on Arbitrum

---

## How to Enable Express Trading?
1. Open [GMX app](https://app.gmx.io/)
2. Click on Setting on the top right of the screen. Navigate to Trading Settings tab and select "Express".
